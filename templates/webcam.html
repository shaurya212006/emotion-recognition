🔒 Privacy & Data Notice — EmoScan v3

Camera Access & Data Usage Agreement

⚠️ This system captures video from your device camera and processes it using AI. You must read and agree to the terms below before proceeding.
1. Data Collection
  • Your camera feed is accessed only after your explicit consent.
  • Individual frames are sent to the server for emotion analysis.
  • No raw video or images are stored permanently.
  • Only the prediction result (Happy/Sad + confidence) is logged.
2. What Is Logged
  • Timestamp of each detection (UTC)
  • Number of faces detected
  • Emotion label and confidence score
  • No biometric data, no face embeddings, no identity information stored.
3. Your Rights
  • View all logs anytime via the Records page.
  • Delete all logs anytime — no questions asked.
  • Stop the camera anytime using the Stop button.
  • Closing this page immediately ends all camera access.
4. Security Measures
  • All data transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS in production).
  • Rate limiting applied to prevent abuse.
  • Security headers (CSP, X-Frame-Options) enforced on all responses.
  • Audit logs with hash-chaining ensure log integrity.
5. Purpose

This is a research and demonstration prototype developed for the IDEMIA internship programme. Not intended for medical, legal, or commercial use.
